Welcome to Roses

Roses Company for Exporting Herbs, Medicinal & Aromatic Plants, and Essential Oils

Since 1999, Roses Company has been a leader in the field of producing and exporting premium-quality herbs, medicinal plants, aromatic plants, and essential oils. Based in Egypt, we are proud to be one of the largest manufacturers and exporters in this industry.

Our commitment to quality is evident in every product we offer, sourced from naturally grown plants in their indigenous environments. By supporting fair trade practices, we ensure that farmers are valued and directly involved in our process.
